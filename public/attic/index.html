Attic

You are in a dusty old attic. It feels like the ghost of an archivist haunts the room.

To the north, an old bookshelf towers, draped in cobwebs. A faded label reads, “Blog posts, pre-July 2026”.

You dread to look, but behind you, to the south, you sense a deadly robot, and a rabid dog, both racing to overtake and devour you.

Shining to your left, in the east, is a gleaming pile of treasure. The rich results of battles past.

A rat scurries past, as you hesitate, wondering which way to go.
